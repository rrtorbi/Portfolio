About me

In the heart of Accra, Ghana, a stranded hustler named Ronald dreams of breaking free from hardship—not with handouts, but with hard work and code. By day, he runs errands and takes on small gigs to survive; by night, he pours himself into learning web development from free online resources and borrowed data. With a worn-out laptop and relentless drive, Ronald envisions a future where he becomes not just a skilled web developer, but a successful tech entrepreneur. Each challenge fuels his determination, and though the journey is tough, he’s committed to building something greater—starting from nothing, but aiming for everything.

As a Data Analyst and Data Collector at Beacon Power Services, I play a crucial role in transforming raw data into actionable insights that drive smarter energy solutions across West Africa. My work involves gathering and cleaning large volumes of energy consumption and distribution data from various sources, ensuring accuracy and consistency. I collaborate closely with engineers and software teams to analyze patterns, monitor grid performance, and identify areas for operational improvement. By leveraging tools like Excel, SQL, and Power BI, I help deliver real-time, data-driven reports that support decision-making and enhance service delivery for power providers and consumers alike.
